📋 Retirement Process Guide

⏰ Important Notice

Please submit this form at least 3 months before your retirement date to ensure smooth processing of all benefits and documentation.

📅

6 Months Before Retirement

  • Attend pre-retirement counseling sessions
  • Collect all necessary documents (PAN, Aadhaar, Bank details)
  • Review your service records and provident fund statements
  • Plan your post-retirement accommodation
  • Update nomination details for all benefits
📝

3 Months Before Retirement

  • Submit this online retirement form with all required details
  • Upload photograph with blue background
  • Provide post-retirement contact address and phone number
  • Choose your mediclaim scheme option (Yes/No)
  • Complete medical examination at company hospital
  • Submit spouse details for dependent benefits
🔍

1 Month Before Retirement

  • Verify all submitted information is correct
  • Attend exit formalities and clearance procedures
  • Return company property (ID card, keys, equipment)
  • Collect retirement order and settlement letter
  • Arrange for handover of your current responsibilities
✅

On Retirement Date

  • Receive farewell and appreciation ceremony
  • Collect final settlement documents
  • Get your retirement benefits account details
  • Receive pensioner identity card
  • Obtain access credentials for pensioner portal
🎉

After Retirement

  • First pension credit within 30 days
  • Gratuity payment processed within 45 days
  • PF settlement within 60 days
  • Mediclaim policy activation (if opted)
  • Access to retiree welfare programs and events
  • Annual life certificate submission (digitally available)
